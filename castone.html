Shinobu Kocho

Shinobu Kocho (胡 (こ) 蝶 (ちょう) しのぶ, Kochō Shinobu?) is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. She is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the current Insect Hashira (蟲 (むし) 柱 (ばしら) , Mushi Bashira?).She is the younger sister of kanae kocho(the current flower hashira) and older sister of kanao tsuyuri,aoi kanzaki and the triplets naho,kiyo and sumi kocho, who help around whenever the can assist her in the butterfly mansion.
